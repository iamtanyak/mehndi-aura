How I work

I do not like making every hand look the same. Before a booking, I usually ask what you are wearing, how much detail you like, whether you want the design to feel soft or bold, and how much time we really have.

For brides, I can build the design more slowly with fuller coverage and personal touches. For Eid or party bookings, I keep the design pretty, neat and comfortable for the time available.

My favourite designs usually have florals, soft curves, dotted trails, mandalas or jewellery-style details. I like designs that feel decorated without looking too heavy.

Most of all, I want you to feel relaxed when you sit down for mehndi. It is a small moment before a big day, and I think that moment should feel special too.
